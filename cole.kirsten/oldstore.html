Our Story

5:20am

myJOOS is built upon the shared belief that it's time to return to eating from the earth. Healthier food that is better for you and better for our planet. We are a husband and wife duo that opened our business 3 years ago to employ our community and feed our neighbors.

We make fresh juices daily from our garden here in Echo Park Los Angeles. The pupl from our juices is then used to fertilize our gardens and promote the next batch of juices.

You can rely on freshest juice in Los Angeles every day, from a garden that grows just around the corner.
