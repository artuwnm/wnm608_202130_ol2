Statement of Intent

Reasons for creating this e-commerce website: When customers are shopping in a jewelry store that has many jewelry displays, they are surrounded by hundreds of other designs all dazzling them at the same time. It can be hard to pick out the right thing from a large selection. Taking that process online, consumers are presented with a curated selection of pieces tailored to their budget and style preferences — all in a few clicks. Also, compared to a physical store, there are some benefits that a jewelry shopping website has:

1. Convenient: People are busy these days, many shoppers have to take many jobs, so there is no time for them to shop in a physical store. But they can shop 24/7 on a jewelry website, there is no time limitation.

2. Variety: As a high jewelry company, it is almost impossible to put all your jewelry collections on display, however, customers can browse all product pictures you have on a website. Price comparison: Comparing and researching products and their prices is so much easier online, shoppers don’t have to ask representatives one by one.

3. Save company budges: Customers moving online means there is no need to hire too many salespeople in the store, which helps your jewelry company save on salary budgets.

Also, it tends to be more chaotic when there are more crowds out and this sometimes makes people feel rush or hurried. All these problems can be avoided when people shopping online.
