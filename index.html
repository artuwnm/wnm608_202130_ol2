Recent news

01/22/2021

On January 21, 2021, ye Chengzhi, deputy to the National People's Congress of Hunan Province and President of Mould Technology Co., Ltd., and Tan Dongsheng, deputy general manager of the company, received a delegation led by Li Yan, deputy director of Zhangjiajie Bureau of industry and information technology, and came to Chang to exchange the relevant work of "provincial industrial design center." Following director Zeng of the company's innovation and Research Center, the delegation visited the company's industrial design achievements exhibition, introduced the company's development history, six types of standard modules, patented technology, parametric design, intelligent application, and application fields of modular new materials, and demonstrated various intelligent sensing and control functions in the exhibition hall on the first floor.

About Us

Hunan Yuegang modco Industrial Co., Ltd. is a global innovative modularized new material high-tech enterprise that takes modularization theory innovation, technology innovation, industrial model innovation, and business model innovation.
